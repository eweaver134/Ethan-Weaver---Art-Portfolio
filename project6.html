Project 6 - Titanaboa Model

The final project was to make any model of our choosing. I had the most fun with the prehistoric animal, so I chose to do another one. This time, I sculpted a Titanaboa, but I used several of the skills that I learned from the other projects. I created the base mesh in Maya, then transfered it to Mudbox to do the sculpting and painting. Lastly, I transfered it back to Maya to render a turntable of my model.
